Why is B OS Different?

B OS was born from a vision to blend beauty, effectiveness, and efficiency, much like the harmonious work of bees. We also embrace global solidarity, bringing freedom and fairness into every step. With a KDE foundation, B OS offers a beautiful, effective and efficient experience for all users.

Key Features of B OS Linux

Photo Printing

Print your photos in multiple sizes with broad printer compatibility. Perfect for home and professional use.

Integrated Antivirus

Keep your system safe with B OS's built-in antivirus. Enjoy real-time protection and easy scanning.

Local Chat

Communicate securely with Bmessenger, the built-in local chat tool for fast, private messaging on your network.

Package Wizard Xdeb

Download all required packages at once for offline installation on multiple computers. Perfect for schools, offices, or areas with limited internet access. Save bandwidth and stay productive anywhere!

Indo Setup (Xdeb Builder)

Indo Setup is a tool to easily create Xdeb packages for Linux. It helps you bundle applications and their dependencies into a single Xdeb file for simple distribution and offline installation.

Easy Maintenance

Apply common fixes and updates through a simple graphical interface. It's the ultimate tool for stress-free system management, making it easy for both beginners and experienced users to keep their systems running smoothly.
