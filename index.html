The Story Behind B OS

B OS is designed to be as helpful and productive as a bee—bringing benefits to everyone. The user interface is inspired by the simplicity and elegance of ChromeOS, making it intuitive and modern.

Under the hood, B OS is powered by Debian Testing, following the approach of Google's gLinux for stability and access to the latest technology. We chose KDE Plasma as the desktop environment because it feels familiar to Windows users, making the transition easy and comfortable.

B OS is crafted for learning, creativity, and productivity—combining the best of open source with a beautiful, user-friendly experience.
