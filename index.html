What is B OS?

B OS is an operating system inspired by the harmony and productivity of bees.

Designed to help you focus and achieve more, B OS combines the simplicity of ChromeOS with the flexibility of Windows, providing a modern and familiar environment.

Built on Debian Testing and powered by KDE Plasma, B OS offers up-to-date technology and a customizable desktop. Every detail is crafted to support your productivity, creativity, and digital freedom.
