What is B OS?

B OS is an operating system inspired by the harmony and productivity of bees.

Designed to help you focus and achieve more, B OS combines the simplicity of ChromeOS with the flexibility of Windows, providing a modern and familiar environment.

Built on Debian Testing and powered by KDE Plasma, B OS offers up-to-date technology and a customizable desktop. Every detail is crafted to support your productivity, creativity, and digital freedom.

Key Features

Live Experience

Try B OS without installation. Explore features safely, anytime.
Features may vary depending on hardware and configuration.

Modern Technology

Based on Debian Testing and recent drivers for speed and compatibility.

Antivirus

Stay safe with a powerful antivirus built using PySide6 and ClamAV. Enjoy real-time protection and an intuitive interface to scan, detect, and remove threats with ease.

Local Chat

Communicate with others on your network using Bmessenger, a built-in chat tool for fast and easy local messaging.

Photo Printing

Print photos in various sizes. Compatible with many printers.

App Framework

Develop and run apps with KDE and PySide6. Access open-source applications.

App Installer

Install software easily. Supports .deb, AppImage, Flatpak, and more.

Easy Maintenance

Maintain your system effortlessly with fixrun—common fixes and updates are available through the graphical interface.

No Cost

B OS is open source and available to download.
No subscription is required for the base system. Some third-party apps may have their own licenses.

Snapshot & Rollback

Create system snapshots and easily roll back to a previous state if something goes wrong.

Remote Access

Access and control your B OS device securely from anywhere in the world.

Unified App Store

Discover, install, and update all your apps from one place—supporting Flatpak, AppImage, and more.
